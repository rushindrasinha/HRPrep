Changing Room

Watch the world change around you!

Changing Room is a mobile-based app which provides the user with an easy alternative to the traditional method of asking for outfit advice.

Typically a person tries on a clothing item, snaps a photo, and then sends it to a group chat of 5 of their closest friends and proceeds to wait all day for their expert critiques.
With Changing Room the user simply takes a photo, uploads it to the app, and within minutes has hundreds of critiques, ratings, and a better idea on whether or not to purchase the outfit.
